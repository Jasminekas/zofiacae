Part 1: Multiple Choice Cloze

Read the text and choose the correct answer (A, B, C or D) for each gap.

Question 1
The discovery of ancient civilizations has _______ our understanding of human history significantly.
Correct answer: B) transformed
Question 2
Despite facing numerous obstacles, she _______ to complete the project on time.
Correct answer: A) managed
Question 3
The committee _______ the proposal due to insufficient evidence.
Correct answer: B) rejected
Question 4
The environmental crisis requires immediate _______ from both governments and individuals.
Correct answer: B) action
Question 5
The financial markets showed _______ improvements throughout the year.
Correct answer: D) steady

Part 2: Open Cloze

Read the text and write the correct word in each gap. Only one word is needed.

Question 6
The technology company announced that it would invest heavily in research and development _______ the next five years.
Correct answer: over (or: during/throughout)
Question 7
Athletes need to maintain a balanced diet in order to perform _______ their best.
Correct answer: at
Question 8
The report suggests that climate change has _______ a significant impact on biodiversity worldwide.
Correct answer: had
Question 9
As far _______ we know, the project will commence next month.
Correct answer: as
Question 10
The museum has _______ home to countless artifacts for over two centuries.
Correct answer: been

Part 3: Word Formation

Use the word given in CAPITALS at the end of each line to form a word that fits in the gap.

Question 11
The _______ of the conference has been postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. COMMENCE
Correct answer: commencement
Question 12
Her _______ approach to problem-solving has earned her several awards. INNOVATE
Correct answer: innovative
Question 13
The _______ of traditional values in modern society is a topic of considerable debate. PRESERVE
Correct answer: preservation
Question 14
The scientist's findings were _______ challenged by members of the academic community. CONSIDERABLE
Correct answer: considerably
Question 15
The government's _______ in the economic crisis was widely criticized. ABLE
Correct answer: inability

Part 4: Key Word Transformations

Rewrite each sentence using the keyword provided so that it means the same. You must use between 3 and 6 words.

Question 16
Original: "I find this book very interesting."
Rewrite: "This book _______" [INTERESTS]
Correct answer: interests me greatly / interests me very much / interests me a lot
Question 17
Original: "She regretted not studying harder for the exam."
Rewrite: "She wished _______" [STUDIED]
Correct answer: she had studied harder / she had studied harder for the exam
Question 18
Original: "It's possible that they will arrive late."
Rewrite: "They _______" [MIGHT]
Correct answer: might arrive late
Question 19
Original: "The traffic was so heavy that we missed the beginning of the film."
Rewrite: "Such heavy traffic _______" [CAUSED]
Correct answer: caused us to miss the beginning / caused us to miss the beginning of the film
Question 20
Original: "Smoking is forbidden in the restaurant."
Rewrite: "Customers _______" [NOT]
Correct answer: are not allowed to smoke / must not smoke
